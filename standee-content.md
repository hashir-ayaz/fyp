## Problem We're Solving

Muslims worldwide struggle to find specific knowledge buried in hours of Islamic lectures. Current platforms offer only basic keyword search, no cross-referencing of Quranic verses and Hadith, and no way to verify AI-generated religious content. With shrinking attention spans, long-form lectures need a smarter, more accessible format.

---

## What Quran For All Does

### 1. Lecture Discovery & Playback
Browse, stream, and download Islamic lectures. Tap any line in the transcript to jump to that exact moment in the audio.

### 2. Multilingual Transcription with Expert Review
AI transcribes lectures in Urdu, Arabic, and English. Al-Huda scholars review and approve every transcript before it goes live.

### 3. Smart Q&A with Citations
Ask questions in natural language. Get answers sourced exclusively from verified lectures — with direct links to the Quran, Hadith, and the exact audio timestamp.

### 4. Islamic Knowledge Graph
Explore how lectures connect to Quranic Ayat, Hadith, and topics. Select any verse to see every lecture that references it.

### 5. Short-Form Content
AI extracts 30–120 second clips from long lectures. Verified by scholars, ready to share on WhatsApp and social media.

---

## What Makes Us Different

- Every AI output is **verified by Al-Huda scholars** before reaching users
- Answers are **citation-backed** — linked to Quran, Hadith, and audio timestamps
- **Reverse lookup**: pick any Ayah or Hadith and find all lectures referencing it
- Built on **30,000 authentic lectures** from Al-Huda International
